# Roadmap

Money Engine AI is an open-source, self-hosted pre-CRM for turning skills into testable B2B offers, 7-day revenue sprints, metrics, and weekly reviews.

## Current Focus

- Keep the local-first Next.js app stable and easy to self-host.
- Improve structured AI outputs for revenue paths, offers, sprint plans, and weekly reviews.
- Grow the community playbook registry with ethical, specific, schema-validated playbooks.
- Add regression tests for prompts, playbooks, outreach safety, and import/export flows.

## Milestones

### v1.0.x - Stable Self-Hosted Core

- Maintain Docker-based deployment.
- Keep SQLite and local-first data ownership as the default.
- Improve documentation for setup, contribution, privacy, and security.
- Keep CI green for lint, tests, playbook validation, and production builds.

### v1.1 - Playbook Quality and Evals

- Add more seed playbooks for narrow B2B service experiments.
- Add prompt and structured-output eval fixtures.
- Expand anti-spam and no-false-claims tests.
- Add clearer contribution review rules for playbooks.

### v1.2 - Dogfooding and Examples

- Publish real sprint reports using the dogfooding template.
- Add screenshots or a short demo video.
- Improve exported sprint and review examples.
- Convert user feedback into public issues and roadmap items.

### v1.3 - Maintainer Automation

- Use AI-assisted issue triage, PR review, release-note drafting, and docs translation.
- Add safer maintainer workflows for validating community playbooks before merge.
- Keep automation focused on review and quality, not spam or bulk outreach.

## Non-Goals

- No income guarantees.
- No bulk outreach or spam automation.
- No fake testimonials, fake case studies, or deceptive sales scripts.
- No full CRM scope until the pre-CRM revenue sprint loop stays simple and useful.
